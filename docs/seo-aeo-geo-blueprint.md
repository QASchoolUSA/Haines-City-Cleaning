#### Cluster A — Lived-In Residential Hygiene

**Semantic center:** Ongoing cleanliness of occupied homes and apartments in Haines City / Polk County.

**Transactional / commercial keywords (3)**
1. house cleaning Haines City FL
2. maid service near me Haines City
3. recurring residential cleaning Polk County

**Informational long-tail questions (3)**
1. How often should a Haines City home get a professional clean in Florida humidity?
2. What is included in a standard vs deep house cleaning in Haines City?
3. Are eco-friendly cleaning products safer for pets and kids in Central Florida homes?

**Intent gap (what competitors miss)**  
Generic “sparkling home” articles ignore **Florida-specific soil load**: AC condensate dust, pollen seasons, hard-water film on fixtures, and humidity-driven bathroom biofilm. Experts specify room-level dwell times, product chemistry for hard water, and a humidity-aware cadence (e.g., bathrooms weekly, deep cleans before peak pollen / hurricane prep), not just “we clean kitchens and bathrooms.”

### 2.1 Three Proprietary Data Injections

Publish a scored matrix from internal walkthrough / callback data (anonymized), e.g.:

| Failure zone | Share of deposit disputes / callbacks | Typical miss | Expert fix |
|---|---|---|---|
| Oven / range interior | 28% | Grease on door glass & burner pans | Degrease + heat-cycle wipe protocol |
| Bathroom grout / shower glass | 22% | Soap scum + hard-water film | Acidic hard-water pass + squeegee finish |

**E-E-A-T signal:** Named sample size (“based on 120+ Haines City / Davenport vacancy cleans, 2024–2026”), photo examples, and before/after inspection notes.

#### Injection 2 — Airbnb Turnover SLA Architecture (exact workflow data)

| Phase | Minutes | Owner | Gate |
